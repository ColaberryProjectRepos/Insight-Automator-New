Radar chart 27




Click on get more visual and search for Radar chart -xViz v3.0.1. Add the visual to your visualizations .

under build visual add ColumnCategory column to the Axis field  and then add Category to Legend field .
under visual --->chart Option make the Global Font Family to SegoeUI and the chart Type (pro) to Radar --->turn the Conditional Formating in legend to ON .

In Axis turn Auto Axis Min /Max to On .Axis Label color =#333333 and Axis label font size
Axis line color =#808080 and Line Width =1.
turn  data label =off.
In the Formatting Tab-->General-->Properties-->Size: Height=284, Width=613, Position, Horizontal= 0, Vertical=436. Title=On, Text=Time Period, Font = SegoeUI, Size = 17, Color = #12239E, Background:#ABD8EE, Center, Effect: Background: On, Color = White, Transparency=0%.
